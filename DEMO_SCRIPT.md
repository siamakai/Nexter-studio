# Nexter AI — VA App Demo Script (5 Minutes)

---

## OPENING (30 sec)

> "This is your AI Virtual Assistant — a second brain that runs your business in the background 24/7. It connects to your email, calendar, CRM, Zoom, and more. Let me show you what it does in a typical day."

---

## 1. MORNING BRIEFING (45 sec)

> "Every morning at 6am you get this:"

**Show:** The daily briefing email / briefing cron output.

Points to hit:
- Today's meetings with attendee names
- Unread emails that need action
- Hot leads from the CRM
- Your top 3 open tasks for the day
- If you have 4+ hours of meetings → it automatically blocks a focus window in your calendar

> "You wake up knowing exactly what matters today — before you open a single app."

---

## 2. CRM — HOT LEADS (45 sec)

**In the chat, type:** `Show me all hot leads`

> "It pulls every contact tagged as hot from the CRM in real time — name, company, email, last activity. You can immediately ask:"

**Type:** `Add a note to [contact name]: followed up by phone`

> "Or: create a task, update a tag, move them to a pipeline stage — all in plain English. No clicking through menus."

---

## 3. INBOX TRIAGE (45 sec)

> "Every 15 minutes the assistant scans incoming emails and categorises them:"

- **Urgent** → you get an instant alert with a draft reply ready
- **Action needed** → a draft reply is saved in Gmail Drafts for your review
- **FYI / No action** → ignored

> "You only touch emails that actually need you. Everything else is handled."

---

## 4. MEETING INTELLIGENCE (60 sec)

> "Every Zoom and Google Meet call is automatically captured:"

**Show:** A meeting report file / Drive document.

- Summary of what was discussed
- Key decisions made
- Action items extracted and added to your task list
- Contact updated in CRM with a note
- Summary emailed to you right after the call

> "If a meeting wasn't recorded, it emails you and asks you to provide notes — nothing falls through the cracks."

---

## 5. SMART FOLLOW-UPS (30 sec)

> "Every morning it scans your sent emails. If someone hasn't replied in 3 days, it drafts a follow-up and saves it in Drafts. You just review and click send."

> "For contacts that have gone quiet — 30, 60, or 90 days — it drafts re-engagement emails tailored to them. Automatically."

---

## 6. WEEKLY REVIEW (30 sec)

> "Every Friday at 5pm you receive a weekly review:"

- What you accomplished
- What's stuck or overdue
- Your top 3 highest-leverage actions for next week

> "So you never start Monday without direction."

---

## CLOSE (30 sec)

> "Everything you just saw — briefings, triage, follow-ups, meeting reports, CRM updates, weekly reviews — runs automatically in the background. You don't configure it. You don't check dashboards. It just works.

> And we build this customised for your business — your workflows, your CRM, your team. Ready to see what it looks like built for you?"

---

## DEMO TIPS

- Keep the chat window visible throughout
- Have a real hot lead in GHL ready to pull up
- Show a real meeting report from the meetings/ folder or Drive
- Demo the briefing email if you have one from this morning
- Total time: ~5 minutes — leave 2-3 min for questions
