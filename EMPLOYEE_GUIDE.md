## Chat Skills (Type These Shortcuts)

### `/brief` — Fresh Briefing On Demand
Get a live briefing at any time of day, not just 6am.

```
You type:   /brief
You get:    A full briefing generated right now — meetings, inbox, leads, tasks.
```

---

### `/meeting-prep [name]` — Prepare for Any Call
```
You type:   /meeting-prep Ahmed Al-Farsi
You get:    Ahmed's CRM profile, last interaction, deal stage, 3 talking points.
```

---

### `/email-draft [what you want to say]` — Write Emails Instantly
The assistant drafts it. You review and approve before anything is sent.

```
You type:   /email-draft follow up with Parastoo, polite but firm, she needs to sign the contract
You get:    A ready-to-send email in your tone. One click to approve and send.
```

---

### `/crm [query]` — Your CRM, Instantly
```
You type:   /crm show me all hot leads
You get:    Every contact tagged "hot" with name, email, company, last contact date.

You type:   /crm add note to Ahmed Al-Farsi: called today, sending proposal tomorrow
You get:    Note saved to Ahmed's record in Go High Level immediately.

You type:   /crm move Lena Müller to warm lead
You get:    Her tag updated in the CRM.
```

### `/content` — Content Calendar
```
You type:   /content list
You get:    All content pieces with status and scheduled dates. Overdue items flagged ⚠️
```

### `/delegate` — Assign & Track Team Tasks
```
You type:   /delegate ask Anna to update the website case studies by May 10
You get:    Task logged: Anna | "Update website case studies" | Due: 2026-05-10

You type:   /delegate list
You get:
  • Anna: Update website case studies | Due: May 10 [assigned]
  • Marco: Draft Q2 client report | Due: May 8 [OVERDUE ⚠️]

You type:   /delegate mark [ID] done
You get:    Task marked complete.
```

If a task becomes overdue, you receive an email alert automatically. The system tracks how many nudges have been sent per task.
